Hollywood Smile

Hollywood Smile is a specialized service crafted to shape and enhance your teeth and smile in a unique manner. Every individual's smile is different, hence the Hollywood Smile is personalized especially for you.

  • Smile Analysis: The first step is the analysis of your smile. The size, shape, color, and alignment of your teeth are meticulously examined. This analysis aids us in determining which modifications are required.
  • Dental Aesthetics: Hollywood Smile encompasses dental aesthetic procedures. This can include teeth whitening, porcelain veneers, braces, and more.
  • Design Based on Personal Needs: Hollywood Smile is crafted based on your personal preferences and goals. If you desire whiter, straighter, and more attractive smiles, this service is tailored just for you.
Hollywood Smile offers a unique smile with the amalgamation of modern dental technology and our expert team. For more information and to transform your smile, please contact us.

Teeth Whitening

Teeth whitening is a quick and effective way to rejuvenate your smile. With our clinical experience and state-of-the-art whitening methods, we assist in whitening yellowed, stained, or dull teeth.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Cosmetic dentistry is a means to make your smile more attractive and aesthetic. With our clinical experience and passion for aesthetics, we are here to achieve the best and most natural results for you.

We adopt a personalized approach for every patient. Initially, we conduct a discussion to understand your desires and expectations. Then, we craft an aesthetic plan suitable for the shape of your face, the size and ratios of your teeth, your tooth color, and the overall appearance.

Our cosmetic dentistry service addresses various aesthetic issues, including color correction of your teeth, alignment of irregular teeth, replacement of missing teeth, and more. We pay attention to the safety and quality of the materials used, ensuring natural and durable results for you.

Combining technology and expertise, we offer you the best service with the latest digital imaging and design systems. Join us in our clinic to discover the art in your smile with cosmetic dentistry.

Dental Veneers

In our dental veneers service, we offer various options to perfect your smile. We consider your wishes and needs to determine the most suitable option.

  1. E-Max Veneers: E-Max veneers are thin and transparent veneers that provide aesthetic results extremely similar to natural teeth. These veneers pass light and ensure your smile looks natural and radiant. They are ideal for both front and back teeth and are known for their durability.
  2. Zirconium Veneers: Zirconium veneers are a superior option, especially in terms of durability and longevity. These veneers are resistant to stains and wear. Aesthetically, they offer quite satisfying results. Zirconium veneers can be used both on the back and front teeth.
Both options are applied with modern dental technology and our expertise, granting you a healthy, natural, and attractive smile. We invite you to our clinic to personalize your smile and determine the best option. Please contact us for more information.

Dental Implants

Dental implants provide a robust and long-lasting solution to replace missing teeth. This service helps you regain a healthy smile and chewing functions by permanently replacing your lost teeth.

  • Personalized Treatment Plan: Dental implant treatment is planned with a personalized approach for every patient. Depending on the condition of your missing teeth and your health history, the most suitable treatment plan is determined.
  • Natural and Durable Results: Dental implants work on the principle of titanium implants fusing with the bone, making them extremely durable. The porcelain teeth placed on them offer aesthetic and function similar to natural teeth.
  • Regain Chewing Functions: Missing teeth can impact chewing functions. Dental implants help you regain your chewing functions by replacing the missing teeth.
Dental implant treatment, with modern dental technology and our expert team, bestows a healthy, natural, and beautiful smile. Don't hesitate to visit us to regain your health and smile. Please contact us for more information.

Gum Diseases

Gum diseases manifest themselves with symptoms like inflammation and bleeding of the gums. If left untreated, they can lead to tooth loss. Early diagnosis and treatment are crucial in overcoming these problems.

  • Personalized Treatment Plan: Every patient's gum health is different; therefore, our treatment plans are specifically tailored for each individual. The first step involves a detailed examination of your gums and identifying the existing issues.
  • Professional Cleaning and Scaling: A fundamental part of gum disease treatment is professional dental cleaning and scaling. We clean tartar and plaque, restoring the health of your gums.
  • Surgical and Non-Surgical Options: Treatment can include surgical and non-surgical options. No matter the situation, our expert team is here to determine the best treatment for you.
Gum health is vital for healthy smiles. Have your gums and overall oral health checked by visiting our clinic. Please contact us for more information.

Maxillofacial Surgery

Maxillofacial Surgery is a surgical branch designed to enhance the health and aesthetics of the mouth, face, and jaw. This service is specially developed to meet various medical and cosmetic needs.

  • Broad Range of Services: Maxillofacial Surgery offers a wide range of services. These include tooth extractions, jaw bone corrections, repair of facial traumas, treatment of jaw joints, and more.
  • Aesthetic Enhancements: Maxillofacial Surgery is employed for facial aesthetics and contouring corrections.
  • Jaw Joint and Chewing Issues: Maxillofacial Surgery is effective in treating issues related to jaw joints. It is used to improve chewing functions and alleviate pains.
  • Personalized Treatment Plan: Every patient's need is unique; therefore, Maxillofacial Surgery treatment plans are personalized. After evaluating your condition, the best treatment options are presented to you.
Maxillofacial Surgery combines modern technology and our expertise to offer a healthy mouth, a beautiful face, and an aesthetic look. Don't hesitate to visit us to enhance your oral and facial health and aesthetics. Please contact us for more information.

Clenching Treatment

Teeth grinding and clenching are common issues that can negatively impact your oral health and overall quality of life. Clenching Treatment is designed to manage this bothersome condition and enhance your oral health.

  • Personalized Treatment Approach: Every patient is unique, and thus, treatment plans are tailored. The first step is to determine the causes and intensity of teeth grinding or clenching.
  • Night Guards (Splints): The cornerstone of Clenching Treatment is night guards. These custom-made devices protect your teeth and help relax your chewing muscles.
  • Improvement in Oral Health: Clenching Treatment also aims to protect your teeth and jaw structure. Post-treatment, it's essential to improve oral health.
Clenching Treatment addresses a common issue effectively while elevating your oral health and quality of life. It promises a more comfortable smile and better night's sleep. For more information about this service, please contact us.
