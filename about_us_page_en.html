ABOUT US

Dt. Fahriye Gül Cura

Dt. Fahriye Gül CURA graduated from Istanbul University Faculty of Dentistry. After a short experience in private practice, she served in various provinces under the Ministry of Health. She retired from the Ministry of Health in 2023 and opened her own clinic. Particularly focused on aesthetic fillings and aesthetic prostheses, Ms. Cura closely follows both domestic and international training. She also implements these new techniques and approaches in her current clinic. Believing that dental practices contain an artistic touch, Ms. Cura has also adopted providing quality treatment as her mission. Our clinic complies with the highest hygiene standards and is meticulously sterilized at every stage. The equipment we use is carefully cleaned and disinfected after each treatment. In this way, we offer our patients a safe and hygienic treatment environment.
